How to add a Boostskilla group:
ios:
Open Telegram.
Click the new message icon on the top right.
New group.
Search for "Boostskillabot" and add it to your group.
Giving the group short name describing the Boostskilla subject for example "Learning python"
On the top right click the group icon to reach to "group info" menu.
Click "Edit".
Add Boostskillabot to the Administrators with full privileges including add  new Admins.
Click Done
Go back to the group chat page and type "/start"
Then click register a group.
That's it you're done! your group will now appear when the bot is asked to list all groups.
